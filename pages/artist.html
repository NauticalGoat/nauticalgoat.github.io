Duncan McFadden

DNCN • wetgoat_ • NauticalGoat

Duncan McFadden is a wearer of many hats. Literally and figuratively. He’s one to walk around town and annoy his friends with details of logos they would have paid little attention. He grew up playing video games and eventually fell in love with graphic design when he needed a look for his budding entertainment career. Now Duncan spends his time doodling logo concepts for businesses that live in his imagination. He’s into illustration, iconography, typography, logo design, live streaming, video content creation, motion graphics, longboarding, and photography.
